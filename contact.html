Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the Dosha quiz?

Our Dosha quiz is based on traditional Ayurvedic principles and provides a general assessment of your constitution. While it offers valuable insights, we recommend consulting with a qualified Ayurvedic practitioner for a comprehensive evaluation.

Can I follow Ayurvedic recommendations if I have medical conditions?

Always consult with your healthcare provider before making significant dietary or lifestyle changes, especially if you have existing medical conditions or are taking medications. Our content is for educational purposes and not a substitute for professional medical advice.

How long does it take to see results from Ayurvedic practices?

Results vary depending on individual constitution, current health status, and consistency of practice. Some people notice improvements in energy and digestion within a few weeks, while deeper changes may take several months of consistent practice.

Do you offer personalized consultations?

Currently, we provide educational content and general guidance through our website. We're working on adding personalized consultation services with certified Ayurvedic practitioners. Stay tuned for updates!

Is Ayurveda suitable for children and elderly people?

Ayurvedic principles can benefit all age groups, but recommendations may vary. Children and elderly individuals should follow age-appropriate guidelines and consult with healthcare providers familiar with Ayurveda before making significant changes.
